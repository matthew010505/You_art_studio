Serigraphy Workshop

A Serigraph is an alternative name for silkscreen printing that was coined to distinguish it’s uses in the fine art world from that of the commercial printing world.

Bottle Art Workshop

Bottle art is an art form that has been practiced for centuries across cultures and continents. It involves the use of glass bottles and other containers as canvases for artistic expression, turning them into intricate sculptures, decorative objects, and even functional tools.

Woodcut Workshop

Woodcut is a relief printing technique in printmaking. An artist carves an image into the surface of a block of wood—typically with gouges—leaving the printing parts level with the surface while removing the non-printing parts.

Textile Printing Workshop

Applied arts and works of art are equally possible in the Textile Printing Workshop, a space for creativity and experimentation. Printing pastes, dyes and chemicals for tooling techniques can be mixed at will, while various devices and machines broaden the range of possible printing methods.
